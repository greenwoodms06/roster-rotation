Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026

Data Storage

Rotation Manager stores all data — team rosters, game lineups, plays, and preferences — entirely on your device using your browser's local storage. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no cookies beyond what the browser uses for local storage.

Export & Import

You can export your data as JSON files and import them on another device. These files are created and transferred by you — the app never uploads them anywhere.

Offline Use

The app works fully offline via a service worker that caches the application files on your device. No network connection is required after the initial install.

Third-Party Services

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts on first use. No other third-party services are used.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on the GitHub repository.
